Futuristic Humans is the first album of Futuristic Grow and refers to the belief that humanity can transcend its current physical and mental limitations through the use of technology, going forward in fields such as music patterns, robotics, and artificial intelligence.


In the future, we may see humans with augmented physical abilities such as enhanced hearing.


It is important to consider these implications and regulate the development and use of such technologies responsibly.
